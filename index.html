How it Works

Both B2B buyers and sellers with medium to high merchandise value will trade their products and requirement on WEM, at a global stage.

Our mission is to help B2B E-commerce traders globally.

There is lot of friction in the global B2B marketplace, where some giants have created a monopoly, scrapping off the rights of small businessmen. We are one of you, to help you.

  • Provide a single marketplace where everyone is equal.

  • Create a new crypto economy of B2B marketplace.

  • Revive the dead economy after Covid-19 pandemic, and give trades a new opportunity.

Why is now the best time for WEM?

It's been more than 8 months that the world has shrunken to it's all time low growth rate. The new norms after the pandemic is revealing new challenges in B2B e-commerce such as Changing purchase patterns, flexible shipping needs, discovery of omnichannels, self service desire of sellers, etc.

India is one of the largest market in the world after China in B2B trade. And just this year, the GOI banned Alibaba, the biggest source of Indian wholesale vendors and dropshippers. Besides, after the changed behaviour of B2B customers, they are shifting to online B2B ecommerce platforms for the obvious ease in experience.

Our Solutions

While there are many challenges that B2B ecommerce brands generally face, we have come up with many technology solutions that can help them to overcome gaps in the customer experience, serve their customers better and increase their market share.

Challenge 1: Changing purchase patterns

Buyers have become receptive to personalization and self-service. The solution is that B2B eCommerce
businesses must rely on the right data when interacting with customers. Our system that can automate
manual processes to improve selling efficiency.

Challenge 2: Flexible shipping options

Before pandemic, 60% of buyers preferred service with convenient shipping options. Now even more buyers
seek flexible shipping options asper their needs. We will provide integration with 3PL provider, this facilitates
the flow of order information and other data between our shipping partner and back-office systems.

Challenge 3: Alternative B2B channels

Besides shifting of B2B selling activities to online platforms afte COVID, it has also opened the door to
lesser-known selling models such as B2B2C and D2C. WEM will have the flexibility to sell not only B2B but B2C and D2C.
It gives brands the ability to customize these selling channels to a specific audience with a targeted and relevant eCommerce experience.

Challenge 4: Omnichannel

Omnichannel means supporting additional experiences that customers are looking for beyond sales. WEM will
support strong and flexible integration with ERP that will combine sellers inventory data with customers data, orders and invoices.

Challenge 5: Self-service

B2B buyers now prefer seamless experiences with least friction. 70% of B2B buyers admit that buying from a
website is more convenient than transacting with sales representatives. Our advanced AI modelling will provide better self service
options about product restocking, managing orders, etc.

Best Features

The versatility feature of WEM marketplace has been designed to suit the professional's needs of sourcing, procurement and easy vendor management. Also, there will be a single onboarding process for buyers to fulfill all their purchasing needs.

Safe & Secure

It is our first priority to provide a top level security in the platform, following all the industry standard security protocols. Your privacy is very personal to us, so we will secure it.

Vendor Management

There is a clear lack of a reliable platform where the vendors feel comfortable in the process. We aim to provide an advance mechanism that will make the vendor management system a cake walk. This not only helps the vendors, but also removes a lot of hussle for the operators of the platform.

Crypto Acceptable

It's been more than a decade for the crypto industry to be there in wide open. But still the adoption of crypto in trade is at a bare minimum. WEM is an initiative in that direction to make crypto more feasible for trade activities, ultimately helping the crypto economy to move north.

Digital Records

It's an eternal pain for the traders to maintain their sales data. WEM will provide a robust dashboard that can maintain all the user's data and analyse them to provide conclusive information. This feature shall help traders to make better decisions in their behaviour.

Horizontal Offering

This marketplace will have products from a wide range of horizontal offering products. The buyers and sellers will get to trade all kinds of products from household to fashion and outdoor items, except some prohibited categories.

Crypto rewards

Both the buyers and sellers on the WEM marketplace will get quarterly rewards in tokens according to their maturity level on the platform. This is offered to motivate the users to remain loyal to the platform.

What is Walt Evolve Mart?

Walt Evolve Mart is a B2B marketplace project to be built with the state of the art technology. One of the few marketplaces in the world to accept cryptocurrency. This project is backed by the brilliant minds in the domains of digital ecommerce, marketing and vendor management.

What is an ERC20 token?

ERC20 is a protocol standard that defines certain rules and standards for issuing tokens on the Ethereum blockchain. The ERC20 has become the gold standard for new crypto tokens and has a lot of benefits like:- Uniformity of tech and protocol standard.- Reduced complexity of understanding each type of token implementation.- Enhanced liquidity of ERC20 tokens.- Reduced risk of breaking contracts.

What type of currencies will be accepted in WEM?

ETH, USD, BTC, LTC will be accepted along with 120+ altcoins. More coins or token will be accepted, as requested by the community.

What will happen to the unsold tokens?

In order to protect the value of the tokens of investors and participants, all unsold tokens will be burnt at the end of the token sale.

When will the WEM token be listed on exchanges?

We are in talks with reputed cryptocurrency exchanges for listing of the WEM token and we expect the WEM token to be listed within 30 days of public sale completion. We will share the names of the exchanges after the token sale is over.
